What is Cybercrime?

Cybercrime refers to any illegal activity that uses computers, networks, or the internet as a primary tool or target. From sophisticated hacking operations to simple social engineering scams, cybercriminals exploit vulnerabilities to steal data, money, and personal information. The digital age has created new opportunities for criminals to operate from anywhere in the world, making cybercrime one of the fastest-growing forms of crime today.
